How This Works

Time Split Calculation

Measures the actual distribution of custody hours between parents based on scheduled events.

  • • Counts only custody-type events
  • • Calculates total hours for each parent
  • • Compares against agreed-upon ratio

Preference Satisfaction

Analyzes how often each parent gets custody on their preferred days.

  • • Compares events to stated day preferences
  • • Accounts for specific date preferences
  • • Tracks violations and satisfaction rates
